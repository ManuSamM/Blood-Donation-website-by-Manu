Donation process:

1) Any person can walk in voluntarily any time round the clock at their own convenience to donate blood in a licensed blood bank.

2) Before donation, a test will be conducted of yours to determine the blood group and after that a simple health checkup to check the
blood pressure and cholestrol levels.

3) If you are detected with any rare blood group like the O+,O-, Rh group, then we request you to please donate your blood as this blood
groups are rarely found.

4) Or else get your account verified in this website and you will have the privilege to give a second chance to someone's life and get
help in your need also.


People who have the privilege to save someone's life are licky. Give a meaning and purpose to your life and start donating blood today!
